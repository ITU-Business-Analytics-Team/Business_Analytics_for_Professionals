Big Data & Business Analytics

A comprehensive program that develops data-driven decision-making competency across a wide spectrum from supply chain to finance, customer analytics to Agentic AI. Participants learn both classical analytics methods and cutting-edge AI technologies such as LLM, LangGraph, and multi-agent systems, designing systems that can autonomously solve business problems.

84 Hours 12 Moduls R + Python Capstone Project
